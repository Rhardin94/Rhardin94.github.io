About Me

I am an Illinois State University graduate with a Bachelor of Science, as well as a Full-Stack Web Development Certification from Northwestern University’s Coding Bootcamp.

I have had a passion for what makes computers tick ever since I first interacted with one; finding out how each system functions and interacts with one another is what propels my desire to work in the technological world.

My hunger for discovering how computers work is why I am most suited as a Back-End Developer. I have experience coding with JavaScript, jQuery, and soon several more back-end focused languages, building interactive browser-based games like Psychic and Crystal Collector. I also taught myself to replace my laptop’s hard-disk drive with a solid-state drive purely from the desire to understand and improve its functionality.

Problem solving is what drives me, and is why I am well-positioned to work in a field that both targets existing problems and also improves upon existing solutions.
